# Dear Myself

Dear Myself is a personal daily reflection diary built with HTML, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. It guides you through a simple check-in flow — your mood, your day, your energy, and your self-care — and closes with a personalized reflection and affirmation. Designed with UIverse components and custom Google Fonts, it delivers a calm, aesthetic, and mindful journaling experience for everyday use.

# Features

- Mood Selector – Choose how you're feeling to start your reflection
- Daily Journal – Write freely about your day in your own words
- Energy Check-in – Track your energy levels for the day
- Self-Care Log – Note whether you took care of yourself today
- Reflection & Affirmation – Receive a personalized closing thought based on your entries
- Clean UI/UX – Calm, aesthetic, and distraction-free design

# Tech Stack

- HTML5 – Structure
- Tailwind CSS – Styling & layout
- JavaScript (Vanilla JS) – Functionality & logic
- UIverse – UI components
- Google Fonts – Custom typography
